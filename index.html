From the Editor

Why the NRV Is Worth Writing About

The New River Valley doesn't make many national headlines. It doesn't have a major airport or a famous skyline. What it has is something harder to manufacture — real communities, independent businesses, and people who chose to stay and build something here.

I'm one of those people. I grew up here, served in the Army, and came back. This site is my attempt to document what makes this region worth the attention it rarely gets.

By Ben "Link" Linkous  ·  Pulaski, Virginia

Written by Link

I'm Ben Linkous — most people around here just call me Link. I'm a retired Army Master Sergeant who grew up in Pulaski, left, served, and came back. The NRV is home in a way that's hard to explain until you've been somewhere else long enough to miss it.

I write about this region because I believe it deserves more attention than it gets. The businesses here, the food, the people who stayed or chose to come — they're building something worth documenting.

This site is personal. No press releases, no sponsored posts. Just honest writing from someone who shops local, eats local, and is invested in what this region becomes.
